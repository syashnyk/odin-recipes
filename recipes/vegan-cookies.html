Vegan Lofthouse Sugar Cookies

Description

Looking to satisfy your sweet tooth with a vegan friendly option? Look

no further than these delicious vegan sugar cookies!

Ingredients

Condiments

  • 2/3 cup Vegan butter

Baking & Spices

  • 2 cups All purpose flower
  • 1/4 tsp Almond extract
  • 1 tsp naking powder
  • 3/4 tsp Baking soda
  • 1 cup Cane sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 Vanilla frosting, Vegan

Oils & Vinegars

  • 1 tsp Apple cider vinegar

"Dairy"

  • 1/3 cup Plant milk
