Vegan Lofthouse Sugar Cookies

Description

Looking to satisfy your sweet tooth with a vegan friendly option? Look

no further than these delicious vegan sugar cookies!

Ingredients

Condiments

  • 2/3 cup Vegan butter

Baking & Spices

  • 2 cups All purpose flower
  • 1/4 tsp Almond extract
  • 1 tsp naking powder
  • 3/4 tsp Baking soda
  • 1 cup Cane sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 Vanilla frosting, Vegan

Oils & Vinegars

  • 1 tsp Apple cider vinegar

"Dairy"

  • 1/3 cup Plant milk

Steps

  1. Begin by mixing the dry ingredients in a bowl and set it aside
  2. Mix your butter and sugar until creamy and then add the vanilla and almond extracts
  3. Add the plant milk and vinegar and mix until it is incorporated - it is ok if there is still some slight separation at this point
  4. Pour the dry bowl into the wet bowl and mix justuntil combined
  5. Let chill for 30 minutes in the fridge
  6. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  7. Take 2 tbsp of dough and roll into balls using your hands until smooth, then place on a baking tray covered in parchment paper 2 inches apart
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are just slightly browned - or, if the middle of the cookie springs back up after being poked they are done
  9. Allow cookies to cool for 5 minutes on the tray before trasnferring to a cooling rack
  10. Once the cookies are completely cooled, add any frosting you like!
