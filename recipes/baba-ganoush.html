Baba Ganoush

Description

Baba Ganoush is an eggplant-based dip from the Middle East which is perfect as an appetizer or to put out during a party. Now you can learn how to easily make

this dish by by following this recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 Medium eggplant
  • Olive oil
  • 2-3 tbsp Lemon Juice (about 1 lemon)
  • 1 Large clove of garlic
  • 2 tbsp Tahini
  • Sea salt
  • Optional: 2 tbsp fresh cilantro, parsley, or basil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to high broil (or medium if you have the ability) and position a rack at the top of the oven

  2. Slice your eggplant into 1/4 inch rounds and sprinkle with sea salt and place in a calendar in the sink to drain any excess liquid.

    After 10 minutes, rinse slightly and then pat dry between two towels.

  3. Arrange on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil and a pinch of sea salt. Roast for 5-10 minutes, turning once or twice, until the eggplants

    is softened and golden brown. Remove from pan, stack and wrap the rounds in foil to lock in moisture - wait 5 minutes.

  4. Peel away most of the skin of the eggplant (a little is OK) and add flesh to a food processor. It shoukd be soft and tender and the skin should come off easy.

  5. Add Lemon juice, garlic, tahini,a pinch of salt and mix until creamy. Add herbs last and pulse to incorporate. Taste and adjust seasonings

    as needed. Sometimes it can be better to add a bit more tahini, some extra salt, etc.

  6. Serve with pita and/or pita chips or veggies (for a gluten-free friendly option), and enjoy!. This dish can last in the fridge for several days if kept covered.
