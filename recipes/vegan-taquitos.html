Vegan Taquitos

Description

If you no longer eat animal products for ethical, health, or any assortment of reasons and feel as if the inability to eat most taquitos is one of the cruelest injustices

in this world, then look no further than this fully vegan mushroom-based taquito recipe!

Ingredients

"Meat"

  • 1 Mushroom Chorizo Filling

Produce

  • 1 1/2 cups Brown lentils
  • 2 Cloves garlic
  • 4 Jalapeno
  • 2 cans Rotel fire roasted tomatoes with green chilies
  • 1 White onion, large

Canned Goods

  • 4 cups Vegatable broth

Condiments

  • 1/4 cup Lime juice
  • 1 Salsa of choice or hot sauce
  • 1 Vegan sour cream

Baking & Spices

  • 1 Cauliflower filling, roasted
  • 1 Potato taco filling
  • 1 Tempeh taco filling
  • 1 Tofu taco filling
  • 1 Walnut taco filling
  • Chili powder, sprinkled

Bread and Bakes Goods

  • 1 package Corn or flour tortillas

Seasoning

  • 2 packages Taco seasoning, vegan
